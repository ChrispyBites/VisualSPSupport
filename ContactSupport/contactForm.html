SharePoint Business Support does not grant access to SharePoint sites. Please contact your site's administrator(s) for assistance. If you do not know the site administrator(s), a list is available on the Help tab; look for the Your Site Collection Administrator(s) button.

If you are a site administrator, please review our policy on Team Site Permissions.

SharePoint Business Support cannot troubleshoot ISPAN issues.

Please contact the Team Member Support at 706-644-1234 or teammembersupport@tsys.com.

Your site administrator can resolve most check in/out issues. If you do not know your site's administrator(s), a list is available on the Help tab; look for the Your Site Collection Administrator(s) button.

If the issue persists, click the No button, below, and provide the URL and document filename.

If you have received an error indicating the maximum site allocation has been exceeded, please contact your site collection administrator(s) immediately.

You can find your site collection administrator(s) in the Help tab > Your Site Collection Administrator(s). It is their responsibility to submit a request for additional allocation. If you are a site collection administrator, click here to submit a request for additional storage.

For other document upload issues, click the No button below and use the form to describe the issue in detail, including the location and filename of the document in question. If possible, please include a screen shot or the exact text of the error you're experiencing.

We get it. SharePoint is big and complicated and, even to us, sometimes overwhelming. Luckily, we've got a lot of great resources to help you out!

  • Take some time to explore the Help tab. Each button on the Help tab ribbon launches an article or short video describing how to do something in SharePoint. It's contextual, so take a look at the Help tab everywhere on your site!
  • If you don't know what you need, and just need some help getting started, try visiting the SharePoint Help Site. You'll find a ton of great content there.
  • We have a wonderful community of helpful users on Yammer. Swing by the SharePoint Users Yammer Group and ask your question.
  • If you think you need consultation or more personal assistance, feel free to Submit a Project Request and we'll schedule some time to work with you directly.

The SharePoint Business Support team can offer a number of customization services to help you get the most out of your team's SharePoint site.

Whether you're looking for workflows to automate your business processes, customized list forms, or you'd like a modern site design, feel free to Submit a Project Request and we'll schedule some time to talk through your requirements.

If you need a new site or site collection, please submit a New Site Request.
